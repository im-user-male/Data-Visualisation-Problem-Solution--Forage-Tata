Questions for the CEO:

How much total income does the company have over a lifetime, on a monthly, quarterly, or yearly basis?
What is the most important key growth market and how to make the most out of it?
Who are the top 10 customers by revenue and what patterns or trends do they show?
Is there any products which have high volume or low volume sales consistently that could suggest potentials or inefficiencies in stock?

What are the top-selling products by volume and revenue and how do they perform across regions?

How are market segments performing over time and can marketing campaigns tap-in on these trends?
